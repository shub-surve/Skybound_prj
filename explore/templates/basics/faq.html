Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our sky adventures

General Information

What types of adventures do you offer?
We offer a wide range of sky adventures including tandem skydiving, solo skydiving courses, paragliding experiences, hang gliding, and hot air balloon rides. Each activity is available at different skill levels from beginner to advanced.
Do I need prior experience?
For most of our beginner experiences, no prior experience is needed. We offer comprehensive training and tandem options with certified instructors for first-timers. Advanced activities may require certification or previous experience.
What are your operating seasons?
We operate year-round, weather permitting. However, certain activities may be seasonal or have restricted hours during winter months. Peak season is from April to October.

Safety & Requirements

What are the age and weight restrictions?
Participants must be at least 18 years old (or 16 with parental consent). Weight restrictions vary by activity: generally up to 230 lbs for skydiving and 250 lbs for paragliding. Height and weight requirements ensure proper fit of safety equipment.
What safety measures do you have in place?
All our activities follow strict safety protocols. We use state-of-the-art equipment that's regularly inspected and maintained. Our instructors are certified with extensive experience and undergo regular training. We also maintain comprehensive insurance coverage.
What medical conditions might prevent participation?
Certain heart conditions, recent surgeries, pregnancy, and severe back problems may restrict participation. We recommend consulting your doctor and informing us of any medical conditions before booking.

Booking & Pricing

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak season. Last-minute bookings may be available but are subject to availability. Group bookings should be made at least 1 month in advance.
What's included in the price?
Prices include all necessary equipment, safety gear, training, and instruction. Insurance is also included. Some activities include video packages. Specialty equipment or custom experiences may incur additional costs.
What's your cancellation policy?
Full refunds are available for cancellations made 72 hours in advance. Weather-related cancellations can be rescheduled at no additional cost. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Preparation & Equipment

What should I wear?
Wear comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing and closed-toe shoes. We provide all necessary safety equipment and jumpsuits. Avoid loose jewelry and secure long hair. Glasses and contact lenses can be worn under our provided goggles.
Can I bring my own camera/GoPro?
Personal cameras are generally not allowed for safety reasons on most activities. We offer professional photo and video packages with specially mounted equipment operated by trained professionals.
How long does each experience take?
Plan for 3-4 hours total time including check-in, training, and the activity itself. Actual flight/jump time varies by activity: 20 minutes for paragliding, 5-7 minutes for skydiving. Training courses may take multiple days.
