Critical Requirements

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Medical Certificate Required

A valid medical certificate is mandatory for all participants. No exceptions will be made.

Medical Certificate Must Include:

  • General fitness assessment
  • Blood pressure readings
  • Heart condition evaluation
  • Statement of fitness for skydiving
  • Doctor's signature and stamp
  • Issue date (must be within last 3 months)

Essential Safety Checklist

  • Follow all instructor commands without hesitation
  • Maintain proper body position during freefall
  • Know your altitude awareness procedures
  • Remember emergency protocols
  • Check all equipment before boarding
  • Stay focused during the entire jump
  • Maintain clear communication with your instructor
