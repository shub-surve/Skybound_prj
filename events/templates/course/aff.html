Program Requirements

Age Requirement

Must be 18 years or older with valid government-issued ID

Medical Fitness

Basic medical self-declaration required. Consult your physician if unsure.

Weight Limits

Weight requirements vary by equipment. Generally 220 lbs (100 kg) maximum.

Training

Complete mandatory ground instruction and safety briefing before first jump.

Your Path to Certification

Ground School

6-8 hours of comprehensive ground training covering safety, equipment, and procedures

  • Equipment familiarization
  • Safety protocols
  • Emergency procedures

First Jump

Tandem jump with certified instructor to experience freefall

  • Pre-jump preparation
  • In-air guidance
  • Landing techniques

AFF Levels 1-7

Progressive skill development jumps with decreasing instructor assistance

  • Stability training
  • Turning and tracking
  • Solo deployment

Solo Certification

Complete required solo jumps and written examination

  • Skills verification
  • Written test
  • License issuance
